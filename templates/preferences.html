What types of stocks do you have interest in?

High Capitalization Stocks

This is the total value of the outstanding shares of a stock.

High Beta Stocks

Beta is a measurement of how volatile a stock is. The higher the beta, the more extreme price fluctuation is. These are less predictable.

Low Beta Stocks

Beta is a measurement of how volatile a stock is. The lower the beta, the less extreme price fluctuation is. These are more predictable.

High EPS Stocks

EPS means earnings per share. It is calculated as a companies profits divided by the amount of outstanding shares of its stock

High Forward EPS Stocks

Forward EPS is the expected EPS for a stock during this quarter.

High P/E Stocks

P/E means price-earnings ratio. This is the share price divided by earnings per share. It indicates how profitable a share is.

High Forward P/E Stocks

Foward P/E is the expected P/E for a stock during this quarter
